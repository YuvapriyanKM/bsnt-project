License & Registration Instructions

  • Apply for learner license on the official website.
  • Take a driving test at the RTO office.
  • Submit ID and address proof documents.
  • Medical certificate needed for heavy vehicles.
  • Register your new vehicle at the RTO.
  • Get the RC (registration certificate) from RTO.
  • Renew your license every 5 or 10 years.
  • Separate licenses for bike and car.
  • Check license status on Sarathi website.
  • Use mParivahan app for digital license.

Citizen Guide Instructions

  • Check license status on official portal.
  • Apply for duplicate license if lost.
  • Use DigiLocker to store DL and RC.
  • Follow RTO rules for every state.
  • Transfer vehicle ownership through Form 29/30.
  • Get Pollution Under Control (PUC) certificate.
  • Understand road signs and traffic rules.
  • Check penalties for rule violations.
  • Use Aadhaar for eKYC process.
  • Carry insurance while driving.
